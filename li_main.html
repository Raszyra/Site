When the World Turns Upside Down...Will They Turn With It?

Alistair Schumacher is drafted into the first world war at the age of 21, while his hometown of Cologne is bombed, killing his mother and orphaning his younger sister. Bereft and mad with grief, she embarks on a harrowing journey to reunite with her only living kin.

Surviving the war is only the beginning, however, and as they journey across the globe, finding healing and forging new bonds, they move through the ashes of their past with help only in the strangest of places.
